Who is Anna Chittick

The author

Anna Chittick is an author, systems creator, and the founder of House of ACIE. She is best known as the author of the Surge Trilogy and the creator of Mythic Intelligence.

She builds frameworks that map the architecture beneath human behaviour, desire, identity, and transformation. She writes about behavioural recalibration, coherence, identity, transformation, and human behaviour.

The protocol app

The Surge Trilogy

Anna Chittick is the author of the Surge Trilogy. The Surge Trilogy is three independent 21 day behavioural recalibration protocols, Money Surge, Body Surge, and Love Surge.

Each protocol introduces a coherence framework. Money Surge introduces Money Coherence. Body Surge introduces Body Coherence. Love Surge introduces Love Coherence.

The Surge Trilogy is now a live app at surgetrilogy.app. Each part of the protocol can be read, listened to, or watched, allowing people to move through the work in the form that suits them. The complete protocol experience is US$63.

Originally written in 2021 and still available globally as books, the protocols guide people through structured behavioural sequences that create change through direct participation.

The trilogy is the foundational educational layer of the ecosystem and the entry point into many of the principles that later expanded into the wider House of ACIE ecosystem.

The territories

The Structure. The Shadow. The Human and Machine.

Anna Chittick's work moves through three connected territories. They shape her speech, writing, tools, experiences, and social media, and form the primary ways people enter her world.

The Structure is the architecture beneath experience: the patterns, frameworks, systems, and tools that make the invisible usable.

The Shadow holds the hidden, rejected, contradictory, or unspoken parts of human experience that influence behaviour, identity, desire, and creation.

The Human and Machine explores the relationship between human experience and machine intelligence: how technology can extend authorship, expression, and creation without removing the human from the centre.

Every piece of work may begin in one territory or move across all three. Together they hold the practical, mythic, embodied, and technological dimensions of the House of ACIE.

The territories are the doorways. House of ACIE is the container. Mythic Intelligence is the method. OSHS is the environment where the work becomes interactive.

Executive summary

The ecosystem

House of ACIE is a digital ecosystem built on a single premise. Experience becomes architecture.

Founded by Anna Chittick, House of ACIE turns lived experience into frameworks, tools, systems, and interactive environments that help people build coherence across money, body, love, identity, and business.

What began as one person working to understand her own life has grown into a connected body of work spanning books, behavioural protocols, digital systems, educational frameworks, and emerging ownership models.

At the centre of the ecosystem sits the Operating System for the Human Soul, a living architecture where people explore, learn, build, own, and contribute through interactive web experiences and digital ownership infrastructure.

The container

House of ACIE

Anna Chittick is the founder of House of ACIE. House of ACIE is the container for the entire body of work.

Every framework, book, tool, system, pathway, and future development lives within this larger architecture.

Each component contributes to a single ecosystem that helps people understand themselves, create from their experiences, and build structures that support the lives they want.

The ecosystem keeps expanding while staying connected through one methodology, one language, and one philosophy.

The methodology

Mythic Intelligence

Anna Chittick is the creator of Mythic Intelligence. Mythic Intelligence is the process of extracting structure from experience.

Mythic Intelligence views experience as a source of information that reveals patterns, behaviours, opportunities, and personal architecture.

The purpose moves beyond improvement. The purpose is authorship.

Through this lens experience becomes a source of creation. Experience becomes architecture.

The environment

The Operating System for the Human Soul

Anna Chittick is the architect of the Operating System for the Human Soul. It is the primary environment through which House of ACIE is experienced.

The Operating System for the Human Soul is a living digital architecture built at the intersection of interactive web platforms and digital ownership.

The system brings together educational frameworks, behavioural tools, interactive experiences, digital assets, community participation, and exploration each person directs, inside one ecosystem.

Traditional web experiences and emerging web3 infrastructure work together behind the scenes, so people engage at whatever level suits them.

OSHS is live at oshs.online and remains under active construction. People can enter the environment now while the architecture continues to evolve.

The system is available at different levels. It opens for free and deepens by choice. The 21 day protocols sit inside it as paths a person follows by choice. The Surge Trilogy and Mythic Intelligence are taught through it.

The system is user led. Each person chooses their own depth, follows their own pathways, and builds their own structures from the frameworks provided.

The role of the system is to provide architecture. Architecture people can explore. Architecture people can build from. Architecture people can contribute back into.

As the ecosystem expands, people may engage with digital ownership, NFTs, ecosystem rewards, and earning opportunities connected to the wider House of ACIE environment.

Access structure

Long term vision

A living architecture

House of ACIE is building an ecosystem where experience becomes architecture. Books become pathways. Pathways become tools. Tools become systems. Systems become environments people enter, explore, contribute to, and help shape.

The long term vision is a living architecture where personal development, business creation, education, digital ownership, and participation sit inside one connected ecosystem.

Every framework begins in lived experience. Every tool emerges from a framework. Every system expands from a tool. Every layer stays connected to the same source.

My lived experience becomes architecture.
