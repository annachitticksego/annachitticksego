### the surge trilogy

The **Surge Trilogy** is three independent 21-day behavioural recalibration protocols: **Money Surge**, **Body Surge**, and **Love Surge**.

The trilogy is now a live app at [surgetrilogy.app](https://surgetrilogy.app). Each part of the protocol can be **read, listened to, or watched**, allowing people to move through the work in the form that suits them.

Originally written in 2021 and still available globally as books, the protocols guide people through structured behavioural sequences that create change through direct participation.

The trilogy is the foundational educational layer of the ecosystem and the entry point into principles that later expanded into the wider House of ACIE architecture.

### the three territories

Anna's work moves through three connected territories. They shape her speech, writing, tools, experiences, and social media, and form the primary ways people enter her world.

- **The Structure** — the architecture beneath experience: the patterns, frameworks, systems, and tools that make the invisible usable
- **The Shadow** — the hidden, rejected, contradictory, or unspoken parts of human experience that influence behaviour, identity, desire, and creation
- **The Human and Machine** — the relationship between human experience and machine intelligence: how technology can extend authorship, expression, and creation without removing the human from the centre

Every piece of work may begin in one territory or move across all three. Together they hold the practical, mythic, embodied, and technological dimensions of the House of ACIE.

The territories are the **doorways**. **House of ACIE** is the container. **Mythic Intelligence** is the method. **OSHS** is the environment where the work becomes interactive.

### the ecosystem — house of acie

**House of ACIE** is the container for Anna's entire body of work. It turns lived experience into frameworks, tools, systems, and interactive environments that help people build coherence across money, body, love, identity, and business.

At the centre sits the **Operating System for the Human Soul** — a user-led living digital architecture built at the intersection of interactive web platforms and digital ownership. **OSHS is live at [oshs.online](https://oshs.online)** and remains under active construction. People can enter now while the environment continues to evolve.

**Mythic Intelligence** is the core methodology: the process of extracting structure from experience. It treats experience as information that reveals patterns, behaviours, opportunities, and personal architecture. The purpose moves beyond improvement. The purpose is authorship.

### access structure

House of ACIE has four depths so each person can engage at the level that suits them:

- **Free tools** — open tools, educational resources, and introductory frameworks
- **The Access** — one-time entry to the ownership layer and future participation opportunities
- **The Edit** — a focused three-day intensive to identify, refine, and restructure existing personal, creative, or business architecture
- **The Build** — a twelve-week collaborative process with Anna that consolidates knowledge, experience, projects, and ambitions into one coherent operating structure
