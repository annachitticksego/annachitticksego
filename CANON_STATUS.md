## Canon update — 23 July 2026

### The Structure

The architecture beneath experience: the patterns, frameworks, systems, and tools that make the invisible usable.

### The Shadow

The hidden, rejected, contradictory, or unspoken parts of human experience that influence behaviour, identity, desire, and creation.

### The Human and Machine

The relationship between human experience and machine intelligence: how technology can extend authorship, expression, and creation without removing the human from the centre.

Every piece of work may begin in one territory or move across all three. Together they hold the practical, mythic, embodied, and technological dimensions of the House of ACIE.

## Editorial and entry system

The three territories are lenses and entry points, not separate brands or temporary campaigns.

- Every speech, piece of writing, social post, tool, or designed experience should be grounded in at least one territory.
- A work may cross two or all three territories when the subject requires it.
- The territory is the doorway into the work; it does not replace the House of ACIE identity.
- **The territories are the doorways. House of ACIE is the container. Mythic Intelligence is the method. OSHS is the environment where the work becomes interactive.**

This creates a consistent path into Anna's world while allowing the form, subject, and depth of each work to change.
